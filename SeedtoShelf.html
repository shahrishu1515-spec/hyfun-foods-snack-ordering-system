SEED TO SHELF

We are driven by a strong commitment to delivering food that meets global quality standards. Every step is handled in-house — from sourcing premium seeds and cultivating crops, to storage, processing, and finally delivering the finest potato products to our customers.

AGRICULTURE

Premium Quality Seed Selection

We source exotic, high-grade European-American tubers from leading Tissue Culture Labs around the world. These tubers are then multiplied through our contract farms located in Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, and North Gujarat.

Our focus on excellence starts at the seed stage, ensuring that only superior genetic material is used for cultivation.

GROWING WITH FARMERS

Expert Training Programs

Farmers receive specialized guidance from qualified international agro-experts, focusing on Good Harvesting Practices (GHP) and post-harvest techniques that help preserve crop quality.

Banaskantha - India's Top Potato Producing Region

North Gujarat has become one of the country’s most prominent potato cultivation hubs, supplying produce to major food processors nationwide. HyFun maintains a strong and highly productive partnership with the growers in this region.

Our Partners in Success

Farming contracts with more than 7,500+ farmers ensure a consistent and dependable supply of produce, enabling assured returns for growers and long-term stability for the entire value chain.

Storage and Processing

From the farms, the potatoes are stocked in our in-house cold storage from where they are moved straight to our processing unit.

In-house Cold Storage

The sub-zero cold storage facility can accommodate up to 1.50 lakh tons of potatoes and is equipped with advanced climate-control systems for optimal preservation.

Climate Control

Advanced climate control systems maintain optimal temperature and humidity levels to preserve potato quality.

Traceability

Every potato entering the facility is instantly recorded in a real-time batch-coding traceability system. This ensures complete accountability at every stage and gives consumers full confidence in the safety of the product they purchase.

Processing Unit

At HyFun, operations and technology come together seamlessly in a fully integrated 20-acre processing facility. The infrastructure features a state-of-the-art automated processing line imported from Europe.

Automated Processing

Fully automated lines guarantee consistent quality and precision.

IQF Technology

Individual Quick Freezing preserves farm-fresh quality

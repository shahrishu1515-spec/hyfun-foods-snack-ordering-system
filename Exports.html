Global Frozen Food Exporters

HyFun is a leading exporter of 100% vegetarian frozen food from India, offering a diverse portfolio of over 25 frozen snack products. The range includes global favorites such as French Fries, Crinkle Fries, Potato Wedges, Hash Browns, and Veggie Patties, along with popular local specialties like Aloo Tikki, Soya Patty, and Sabudana Patty. The portfolio is further enhanced by the Puffets range, available in Italian, Mexican, and Schezwan flavors.
